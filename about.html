About

Utility Technology Briefing is a public information site focused on technologies shaping modern water utilities and critical infrastructure organizations.

Coverage areas include operational technology cybersecurity, geographic information systems, artificial intelligence, asset management, customer platforms, enterprise systems, and utility modernization initiatives.

The site is intended as a concise professional briefing for readers interested in the intersection of utility operations, digital platforms, cybersecurity, and emerging technology.

Utility Technology Briefing aggregates publicly available information related to water utilities, operational technology cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, GIS, asset management, and enterprise technology.
